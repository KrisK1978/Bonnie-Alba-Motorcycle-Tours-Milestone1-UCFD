Scotland.....a perfect place for motorbiking!!!

If you are a motorbike enthusiast and looking for a new, exciting adventure...you came to a right place! Scotland is considered as one of the top motorcycling touring destinations and can offer you an unforgettable experience. It has all the essential ingredients for the perfect road trip...literally thousands of miles of quiet yet beautiful roads but first and foremost friendly people. The roads can vary in length and your trip can be as short or as long as you like. You can explore a couple of regions over a weekend or easily spend at least a week touring most awesome sights.

Visit a mystery Dunnotar Castle located on the beautiful eastern coastline, discover mesmerising Lochs and breath-taking scenery. You can also taste a real Scotland by visiting local distilleries, trying the famous Haggis and spending a night in Highlands by the camp-fire... You will find here a number of useful 'hints & tips' about travelling in Scotland and recommended places to visit. If you want to experience a great journey choose Scotland...a truly biking country!
