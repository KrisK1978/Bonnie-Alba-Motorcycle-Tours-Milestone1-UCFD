Places worth to visit...

It does not matter if you have 2 days or a week to explore Scotland...it is always worth it. Scotland has a lot to offer...from week long trips around the famous North Coast 500 or the stunning sites of the Borders. Do not wait and bring your bike to enjoy the fantastic scenery, quiet roads and above all the unforgettable experience!

Discovering North Coast 500

Tour Distance & Duration

513 miles (826km) / 13 hours & 47 minutes

Tour Description

This is one of the most famous and interesting routes in Scotland and it is called a new 'Route 66' and we highly recommend it. The route naturally follows the main roads across coastal edges of the North Highlands taking you through beautiful villages and towns like Ullapool, Durness, John O'Groats, Dornoch & Inverness.

The Whisky Experience...

Tour Distance & Duration

208 miles (331 km) / 5 hours & 35 minutes

Tour Description

Start your journey from a spectacular Cairngorm National Park and visit the beautiful places like Aviemore, Grantown on Spey or Tomintoul. There is a number of the famous whisky distilleries en route where you can find out more about the famous "Angels share", learn about distilling and taste the local cuisine.
